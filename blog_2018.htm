Village Blog : 2018

The following provides a flavour of what's happened in and around Church Fenton in 2018.


March 2018

Church Fenton Community Shop

Church Fenton Community Shop has just won in the Village Shop/Post Office category in the 2017 Countryside Alliance Awards. Well done to all involved.

The Community Shop is run by a dedicated group of volunteer villagers for the village and surrounding areas and has proved to be a huge success and very popular, selling a good selection of foods and other everyday items.
